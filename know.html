UX/UI

Know. is a social network that aims to create a healthy community where not knowing basic things about daily life isn't taboo. On Know., you can not only ask anything without fear of judgment, but it also fosters a spirit of solidarity by helping others.

This simple and easy-to-use app is suitable for all users, and includes video tutorials to make knowledge more accessible and enhance the user experience. It's geared towards young adults who have recently moved out on their own and older adults with life experience, where everyday knowledge is shared. The goal is twofold: to learn about the little details of daily life and to help others with their questions.
